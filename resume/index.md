My research focuses on computer vision, and fashion-oriented generative AI. My current research interests include intelligent garment generation, style transfer, controllable image generation, and virtual try-on.

I am also interested in audio/video generation and AI-assisted video production. Recently, I have started reading related papers and experimenting with basic AI video generation workflows.

In the short term, I aim to quickly read and reproduce valuable research papers, strengthen my understanding of generative models and multimodal AI, and improve my ability to integrate useful modules into practical projects.

In the long term, I hope to develop agentic AI systems that can support professional creative design workflows, enabling more controllable, structured, and production-oriented content creation. I am also exploring the possibility of creating AI video content for social media and self-media platforms.
